About

The kind of strategist who started on a factory floor.

IIT Kanpur · B.Tech Mechanical Engineering
Minor — Industrial & Management Engineering

I started where most strategists don't — on a production floor at MP Birla Cement. I learned early that waste is never random. There's always a data point behind it, and a lever that moves it. A packaging material change I recommended cut moisture absorption by 5%. Small number, real consequence.

"I don't optimise for outputs in isolation — I optimise for the decisions that produce better outputs, consistently."

At Indkal Technologies, I worked directly with the CEO on a fundamental business model pivot — from inventory-led to marketplace. I analyzed 25,000+ TV returns worth ₹1.1B, redesigned supply chain checkpoints, and led the Diwali campaign that delivered 30% national revenue growth.

Today at DriveX, I work across the full stack of used two-wheeler strategy: pricing, procurement planning, competitive benchmarking, and inventory management. I automated the pricing workflow entirely in SQL — what took hours now takes minutes.

I'm an engineer who speaks fluent P&L, thrives on messy datasets, and believes the best strategy is the one that actually gets executed.
